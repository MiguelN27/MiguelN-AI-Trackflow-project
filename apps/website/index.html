What we are building

Core systems prioritized by TrackFlow Tech

Unified Inventory API

Real-time stock visibility for any SKU across both warehouses and automated low-stock alerts.

Carrier Intelligence

Smart carrier selection and unified tracking based on destination, weight, urgency, and past performance.

Reverse Logistics Automation

Rule-based return approvals, automated collections, and AI-assisted product condition classification.

Bilingual CX Automation

Spanish and English first-line support for tracking and returns, backed by unified ticketing and sentiment insights.
